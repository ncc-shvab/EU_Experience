National UK press coverage for EXPERIENCE

May 2021

The Cornwall region of EXPERIENCE has been featured by two national media outlets, thanks to its work on a new off-road cycling route.

It was highlighted in an article in the national newspaper The Guardian, and on BBC Radio 4’s PM programme.

Sophie Gordon from Cycling UK talked about the West Kernow Way in Cornwall – due to open in September - and the plans to introduce more off road routes in Norfolk and Kent as part of EXPERIENCE to extend the visitor season and welcome people to take in the history, culture and landscape of these areas.

  • Read the Guardian article here.
  • BBC Sounds website/app - BBC Radio Four - PM programme - 04/05/2021 - go to: 45mins 15secs – 49mins

EXPERIENCE is a €24.5 million project co-financed by the European Regional Development Fund (€16.9 million) through the Interreg France (Channel) England Programme. It is led by Norfolk County Council and runs from September 2019 to June 2023.
