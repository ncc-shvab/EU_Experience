EXPERIENCE is a €24.5 million project to attract visitors in the off-peak season through a new ‘experiential tourism’ approach. Explore our six regions using the map.

Norfolk

Offers remote, protected wetlands, historic attractions and royal heritage

EXPERIENCE is a €24.5 million project co-financed by the European Regional Development Fund (€16.9 million) through the Interreg France (Channel) England Programme. It is led by Norfolk County Council and runs from September 2019 to June 2023.
